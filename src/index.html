FAQ

  • How much do you pay?

    You can earn 80 cents per mile

  • Weekends home?

    If you run at least 2500 miles a week we can get you home on weekends

  • Can I take the truck home?

    Yes, you can take the truck home for the weekend and immediately head out on the new route on Monday

  • Where do you run?

    We do Midwest, Southern and Eastern states, no NYC (no West Coast). At the bottom of the page, there is a map where the zone of our work is highlighted

  • Can I take a family member/pet on a trip with me?

    Yes, we have a liberal rider/pet policy

  • Where do you hire?

    Primarily IN, OH, MI, IL, PA. But we also hire from other states if those are based close to main highways

  • Is team drive allowed?

    Not yet

  • What kind of freight do y'all haul?

    Everything that can be transported on flatbed trailers: tubes, steel, lumber, etc

  • How often does the cargo need to be tarped?

    50%

  • Vacations and benefits?

    Week after a year, after 60 days - benefits (health, dental, vision and life insurance; 401k)

  • Can I get CDL training from you?

    Currently, we do not offer CDL training for Drivers, but we can recommend courses. We offer flatbed training for experienced Drivers
